Welcome to Wasabi!

Join us on Discord!

Wasabi is a monthly wiped server that wipes on the first Thursday of every month. The decay rate in Wasabi is very low, but it still exsists, so just be sure to keep your tool cupboard stocked to prevent any decay! The gathering rate on Wasabi is set to 2x, meaning you will receive double from gathering than what vanilla Rust would give you. Wasabi is a PvP server, but it is also very much PvE, with plenty of fun PvE mods to keep players on their toes! Kill on sight IS allowed!

Server Mods:

  • 0.25 Decay
  • 2x Gather
  • Radiable Bases
  • Zombies Spawn at night
  • Quests
  • Increased Stack Size on gathering recourses
  • ZLevels Leveling system
  • Storage Sorting
  • Increased Stacking in Chests
  • NPC Drop Gun
  • Player Rankings
  • Quick Sort
  • Shared Authorization with Teammates
  • Server Ticketing System
  • Discord Server*
  • Vehicle Locks
  • Server Bounty System

Player Rankings:

To check your rank and current active play time enter "/ranks" in the game chat (can be team or global).

Loyalty perks are given to best tasting Wasabi.
